About the author
Hal Elrod (born May 30, 1979) is an American author, speaker and success coach.[1] He is the author of the Miracle Morning series, including The Miracle Morning and The Miracle Equation, and the host of the Achieve Your Goals podcast. In 1999, he was involved in a serious automobile accident which he later recovered from.[2] He was born in Camarillo, California.
